"Chapo Ndengu" recipe

Description

Chapati Ndengu is a popular Kenyan dish that is made by combining chapati and green grams.

It is a delicious and nutritious meal that is easy to prepare and can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Chapati
  • Green grams
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Curry powder

Steps

  1. Boil the green grams until they are soft
  2. Chop the onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger and coriander
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add the onions, garlic and ginger
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until they are soft
  5. Add the curry powder and salt
  6. Add the boiled green grams and let them cook for a few minutes
  7. Chapati Ndengu is ready to be served
